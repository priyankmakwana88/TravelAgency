Destinations & Night Stays

> Munnar, India - 2 Nights
> Periyar, India - 2 Nights
> Cochin, India- 1 Night

Itinerary Highlights


Itinerary Code : 190219011

Day 1 > Cochin – Munnar (2 Nights)

Arrive in Cochin, meet the group and proceed to Munnar. ‘I Fly Tour’ Guests will arrive at Cochin Airport as per scheduled flight, on arrival they will meet Veena World Tour Manager at Cochin Airport and we will drive to Munnar by coach or small vehicle, Welcome to Munnar. Today we have a short Meet n’ Greet session before dinner.

Meals >> Dinner

Day 2 > Munnar

Today we enjoy speed boat ride at Mattupetty Dam near Sunmoon Valley and view the Tea Gardens. You can buy flavoured tea packs at Tata Tea Outlet. Later visit Echo Point and Eravikulam (Rajamalai) National Park/ Tea Museum.

Meals >> Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Treat >> Pineapple slice at Sun moon Valley, Mirchi Pakoda at Echo Point, Tea/Coffee/ ice Cream at Eravikulam/ Tea Museum

Day 3 > Munnar > Periyar (Thekkady) (2 Nights)

Today we proceed to Periyar. On arrival enjoy the traditional Kairali show in the evening.

Meals >> Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Treat >> Tea/ Coffee at Nedumkamdan

Day 4 > Periyar (Thekkady)

Today we visit Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary by boat in Periyar Lake. Free time for shopping at Kumily Spice Village.

Meals >> Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Treat >> Tea/ Coffee/Soft Drink at Pullupara and Banana Wafers at Kumily village.

Day 5 > Periyar (Thekkady) – Cochin (1 Nights)

Today we proceed to Cochin. En route we enjoy enjoy the famous Kerala Backwaters ride from Pallathurthy to Alleppey.

Meals >> Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Treat >> Tetra Packed Juice and Pineapple Slice during backwater ride

Day 6 > Departure from Cochin

Today we visit Chinese Fishing Nets followed by visit to Jewish Synagogue and Dutch Palace (subject to Operation) and St. Francis Church at Fort Kochi. Its time now to say goodbye to our tour companions. Let us be in contact with each other through email, Facebook…. And meet again on one more memorable tour. See you all !! ‘I Fly Tour’ Guests will be dropped at Cochin Airport as per scheduled flight.

Meals >> Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Treat >> Coconut water at Jewish Synagogue
